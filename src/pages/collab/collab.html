Collaboration Roles

Client

Responsible for full engagement during the Think Phase and sign-off at the end of the Design, Collaboration, and Transformation Phases

Partner

Responsible for full engagement during the Think Phase, agreed interaction during the Design, Collaboration, and Transformation Phases, and sign-off at the end of each phase

Contractor

Responsible for delivering value at either the Design, Collaboration, or Transformation Phase
